Hola Amigos,

Have you ever thought why there is a hype towards Quantum Computing in todayâ€™s world, and how Materials Scientists could contribute towards developing Quantum Computers?

Quantum Computing is our future. But a lot of research and improvements still need to be done in Quantum World. Quantum Computing needs Materials Science and the use of Quantum Computers can transform Materials Science.

So, let us dive into the world of MATERIALS SCIENCE IN QUANTUM COMPUTING.

Quantum Computing focuses on the development of computer technologies based on principles of quantum theory. Quantum Computers are designed to solve complex problems that today's most powerful supercomputers can't solve and never will.

Qubits are the fundamental but most important unit of data in Quantum circuit. They are used to carry out calculations in Quantum circuit. Most important property of Qubits is that they can be in superposition of energy states, which make Quantum Computers very fast and accurate. TODAY'S BIGGEST CHALLENGE FOR MATERIALS SCIENTISTS IS TO MAKE EFFICIENT QUBITS WITH LONG COHERENCE TIMES! So, if we are able to make efficient Qubits, it will bring a huge revolution in the Quantum World.

There are many materials used in Quantum Computing like Semiconducting, Superconducting materials etc., and even there are some new materials like Semiconducting nanowires evolving for making topological qubits due to their advantages over normal qubits. But there are many challenges associated with these materials which we need to overcome and it is the major aim of Materials Scientists.
